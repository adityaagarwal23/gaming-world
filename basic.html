Some Pros and Cons of gaming

Pros:

  • Increase literacy and understanding.
  • Financial independent at young age.
  • It may help ease anxiety and depression

Increases literacy and understanding :

Gaming increases literacy and ability to understand new things. It teaches how to have patience and control on ourselves in the tough situation, it also teaches us different languages like there are some dialogues in games which increases our vocabulary and also boost our confidence to interact with new people and share our thoughts with them.

Financial independent at young age :

Yes it is true that gaming can generate you revenue and can make you financially independent at young age. You can generate money by streaming on platforms like- twitch, youtube and many more or you can play paid tournaments. Moreover depending on your gameplay sometimes you also get sponsored and paid high revenue by the company.

It may help anxiety and depression :

Narratively and scientifically, video games reduce the symptoms of anxiety and depression. For example, Scientific American reports that the game of Tetris actually reduces the symptoms of post-traumatic stress disorder. In forums like Geek and others, the authors explain how video games can help people with social anxiety disorder learn how to start relationships and learn about social references.

Cons:

  • Video games can become more addictive.
  • Gaming can compromise personal health.
  • It isolates people from families.

Video games can become more addictive :

Nowadays, it is said that gaming addiction is bigger than drug addiction. This addiction can become a big reason for your failure. According to the University of New Mexico, recent studies suggest that 6 to 15 percent of all gamers exhibit signs that could be characterized as addiction. People looses their mindset and are not able to come out of gaming world.

Gaming can compromise personal health :

Gaming addiction can slow down the brain growth, laziness in body, affects eyesight and also results in Insomnia. It increases risk of obesity, high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, and high cholesterol from a sedentary lifestyle and lack of exercise. Let's take an example of well known game - PUBG. Due to this game many young youth has commited suicide because there parents were not able to buy them new device or refuge them to play game.

It isolates people from families :

Some people play video games for many hours like around 10 to 12 hours in a day, sitting at one position in one room without talking to any family member, this isolates people from their loved one's and can also their relationships. Slowly you feel better to play more rather than talking to your families.
